# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This repository is maintained as a technical workspace.
Discussion should stay factual, respectful, and task-focused.

## Expected Conduct

- Use clear and professional language.
- Keep feedback specific to code, documentation, or process.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalating.
- Document technical concerns with evidence when possible.
- Respect different levels of experience and domain knowledge.

## Unacceptable Conduct

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment.
- Discriminatory or abusive language.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of technical results or project status.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, lock, or remove content that does not
follow these rules. Repeated or serious violations may result in
restricted participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to `office@wpowertech.ro`.
Include links, timestamps, and a short factual summary.
